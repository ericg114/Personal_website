My Projects

These are projects I've done related to coding

NYU ARISE Project

With my mentor's guidance, I enhanced my coding skills and gained a deeper understanding of what it means to work in a research environment. In the lab, my partner and I created a MATLAB simulation that can simulate a chemical process for producing adiponitrile, a Nylon 66 precursor used in many plastics. This alternative process has a lower carbon footprint than the traditional process.

Columbia University ENG

Under the guidance of my mentor, Qiang Guo, a postdoc, and Chris Boyce, a professor in charge of the lab, my partner and I were onboarded and trained in the use of different software, such as MATLAB, MFIX, and MobaXterm, to research how active living forces affect structures and chaotic bubbles. We were able to learn professional software through tutorials and the Terremoto supercomputer, which was a major milestone for us. We were also trained to use an MRI and even got to use it for experiments.
